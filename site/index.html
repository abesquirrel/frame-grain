About FRAMEGRAIN

FRAMEGRAIN is a community database of Fujifilm film simulation recipes — carefully crafted in-camera JPEG settings that emulate the look of classic film stocks, cinematic grades, and photographic aesthetics.

Every recipe is validated against the hardware constraints of all five X-Trans sensor generations, so you always know exactly which cameras can run a given recipe.

The "Film Look" Tuning Standards

The Film Look: High WB Shift (Red +3 to +5, Blue −5 to −8), DR400, and Noise Reduction −4.

The Leica Look: Monochrome or ACROS, Shadows +3, Sharpness +2. High-acuity reportage feel.

The Portra Look: Classic Chrome or Astia, overexposed +0.7 EV, warm/gold WB Shift. Soft and flattering.

Cinematic Teal–Orange: Classic Negative, WB Shift Red +2 / Blue −6, Color Chrome Strong.
